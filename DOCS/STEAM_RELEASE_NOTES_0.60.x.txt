[h1]Beta is here: v0.60.0 through v0.60.2[/h1]

The alpha is over. Beta has begun.

[h2]The Wipe[/h2]

The online server database was wiped at beta launch. Every alpha-era character, kingdom, gang, marriage, and bounty is gone. Single-player saves are completely unaffected, they live on your machine.

[h2]Founder Statues[/h2]

Eleven alpha-era founders were commemorated in stone before the wipe. Each statue carries unique ASCII art, a personalized inscription, and an achievement tag. You'll find them in three permanent locations:

[list]
[*][b]Pantheon, Hall of the Ascended[/b] (immortal-tier access)
[*][b]Castle Courtyard[/b], open to royal and commoner alike
[*][b]Temple, Hall of the Ascended[/b] (mortals' path mirroring the Pantheon)
[/list]

You can also visit the hub directly with [i]/founders[/i], [i]/statues[/i], or [i]/hall[/i]. Two founders deleted their own characters before the wipe, their statues are visibly cracked with appropriately ironic inscriptions.

[h2]GMCP Support: Modern MUD Clients[/h2]

If you play Usurper Reborn through [b]Mudlet, MUSHclient, TinTin++[/b], or any other modern MUD client, your status panes now actually work. Live HP/MP/SP gauges, room info, character status, chat capture, all delivered as structured data over GMCP instead of plain text scrolling.

Connect via [i]play.usurper-reborn.net:4000[/i] from any GMCP-capable client and the negotiation is automatic. SSH, web terminal, BBS, and the Electron client are unaffected, you get the same plain-ANSI experience you always had.

[h2]Death System Overhaul (Online Mode)[/h2]

Online characters now have [b]3 free resurrections[/b]. Each death decrements the counter and full-heals you to 50% MaxHP. When the counter hits zero, the next death is [b]permanent[/b], your character is erased forever, a server-wide announcement goes out, and your name joins the news feed.

No menu, no prompts, no save scumming. Single-player still uses the existing penalty options (Temple, Deal with Death, Accept Fate).

If you believe a permadeath was a bug, contact an admin within 7 days. Your save is archived and can be restored.

[h2]Tank Rebalance: No More Walls of Flesh[/h2]

Two issues stacked into one big problem with tank parties:

[olist]
[*]A bug made AoE taunts cast by NPC tank teammates lock monsters onto the tank for the [b]entire fight[/b] instead of the configured 3 rounds.
[*]Even when working correctly, a 100% guaranteed taunt + Shield Wall Formation's 30% damage reduction made the rest of the party untouchable.
[/olist]

[b]Both fixed in v0.60.2.[/b] Class AoE taunts (Thundering Roar, Shield Wall Formation, Divine Mandate, Rage Challenge, Undertow Stance, Abyssal Anchor) are now [b]75% sticky per round[/b]. The tank still draws most of the heat, but about 25% of attacks slip through to the rest of the party. Your healers actually have to heal now.

Hard taunts unchanged: the basic [T] action and Eternal Vigil's invulnerability cooldown still 100% lock targets, as designed.

[h2]Royal Guards Don't Kill You Anymore[/h2]

Failed murder attempts used to cause a "phantom arrest", no combat, no consequence, just a debuff and a teleport to prison. Players hated it. Now: [b]the murder happens[/b], and the Royal Guards arrive for the fight. If you win, you escape. If you lose, you're subdued (HP brought to 1) and hauled to prison without dying. [b]Losing to arrest combat no longer consumes a resurrection.[/b]

[h2]Electron Graphical Client (Beta)[/h2]

The graphical client is still very much in beta, text mode is the supported way to play, but Phase 9.5 polish landed:

[list]

[h2]Skipping 0.58 and 0.59[/h2]

The 0.58.x and 0.59.x version numbers were originally reserved for the Electron graphical client roadmap. That work is now folded into beta, and we jumped straight to 0.60.
